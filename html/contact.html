Booking & Contact Info

Packages Available

Each package includes a set number of images, with the option for clients to purchase additional images or custom products (such as canvases or prints) at an additional cost. Clients can book their sessions directly from the photographer's website, and can upgrade to a higher package at the time of booking for a discounted rate. By offering three different tiers with different services and price points, the photographer can cater to a wide range of clients while still maintaining control over their workload and income.

Pricing Information

Booking a session with us is easy and hassle-free. Simply choose the package that best fits your needs and fill out the contact form on our website. We'll get back to you within 24 hours to confirm your session and schedule a date and time that works best. A deposit will be required to secure your session, with the remaining balance due on the day of the shoot. If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.
